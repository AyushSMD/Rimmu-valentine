## Finding the Perfect GIFs for Your Valentine's Site

### For `proposal.gif` - The "Will you be my Valentine?" Scene

**What to look for:**
- Cute anime/cartoon characters proposing
- Someone holding flowers or a heart
- Kneeling with a ring/gift
- Heart animations
- Romantic gestures

**Recommended Giphy searches:**
1. "anime proposal cute"
2. "cartoon asking valentine"
3. "will you be my valentine gif"
4. "cute proposal animation"
5. "romantic proposal cute"

**Good examples:**
- Anime boy offering flowers
- Character with hearts around them
- Cute proposal with sparkles
- Someone nervously asking with hearts

**Tips:**
- Choose something sweet, not too serious
- Animated style works better than realistic
- Should convey hope and romance
- Avoid anything too dramatic

### For `crying.gif` - The "Please Please Please" Scene

**What to look for:**
- Cute crying characters
- Puppy eyes/begging expression
- Sad but adorable reactions
- Pleading gestures
- Anime-style tears

**Recommended Giphy searches:**
1. "anime crying cute"
2. "puppy eyes begging"
3. "sad please gif"
4. "cute crying please"
5. "anime tears kawaii"

**Good examples:**
- Anime character with big tears
- Cute begging expression
- Sad puppy eyes
- Character holding their heart sadly

**Tips:**
- Should be sad but still cute
- Not too depressing - keep it light
- Puppy-dog eyes work great
- Avoid anything too dramatic or scary

## üì• How to Download GIFs from Giphy

### Desktop Method:
1. Go to [giphy.com](https://giphy.com)
2. Search for your GIF using the terms above
3. Click on the GIF you like
4. Look for the "Download" button (or three dots menu)
5. Select "Download as GIF"
6. Save to your computer
7. Rename the file to either `proposal.gif` or `crying.gif`
8. Move it to the `public` folder

### Alternative Sites:
- [Tenor.com](https://tenor.com) - Good for anime GIFs
- [Gfycat.com](https://gfycat.com) - High quality GIFs
- Google Images - Filter by "GIF" type
