# ADD YOUR FILES HERE

Place these files in this `public` folder:

1. **proposal.gif** - A cute proposal GIF
2. **crying.gif** - A crying/sad GIF  
3. **couple-photo.jpg** - Your photo together

## Where to Get GIFs:

Visit https://giphy.com and search for:
- "proposal cute" or "will you marry me cute"
- "crying sad please" or "puppy eyes begging"

Download the GIFs and place them here with the exact names above.

## Your Photo:

Choose your favorite photo together and save it as `couple-photo.jpg`

The photo will be displayed on the final page with heart decorations!
